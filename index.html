About

Breathing life into characters — one line at a time.

I'm a passionate comic character designer with a deep love for storytelling through expressive illustrations. From bold heroes to quirky sidekicks, I specialize in creating unique and memorable characters that resonate with audiences.

My design process blends creativity and structure — starting with pencil sketches and refining them digitally to match the tone, style, and personality each project demands. Whether it’s for comics, games, marketing campaigns, or web content, I bring characters to life that fit your vision.

Tools of the trade? I work with a combination of Adobe Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, Procreate, and Blender — supported by strong foundations in character anatomy, visual storytelling, and design thinking.

Expertise

What I bring to every character I create.

From sketch to final render, I specialize in designing expressive and original comic characters that enhance stories, games, and brands. My work blends creativity with technical skill, backed by years of hands-on experience.

Character Design

I specialize in crafting distinct, expressive characters tailored to comic books, webtoons, animation, and branding. My designs are rooted in strong silhouettes, personality-driven poses, and clear storytelling.

Visual Storytelling

Every character tells a story — I ensure their visual identity supports the tone, emotion, and world they belong to. From character arcs to scene presence, my designs support engaging narratives.

Digital Illustration
