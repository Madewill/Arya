About Hi, I'm Arya

Crafting Worlds One Character at a Time

I’m a character designer and comic artist who believes in stories that speak louder than words. Whether it’s a brooding warrior with a cursed sword or a goofy alien lost in a supermarket, I bring them to life through expressive lines, thoughtful details, and just the right splash of humor.

How I Got Here

My journey started with doodles in the margins of school notebooks—characters with too much sass and dragons with too many teeth. Over time, those scribbles evolved into full-blown illustrations and graphic stories. I studied technique, devoured visual storytelling, and explored every chance to bring emotion, movement, and depth into a single frame.

Now, I work with individuals, brands, and storytellers who want more than just “pretty pictures”—they want characters that breathe, worlds that pulse, and visual narratives that stick.

What I Stand For

Originality

Cookie-cutter isn't in my vocabulary. Every project is a chance to explore something new—visually and emotionally. I design characters with quirks, flaws, and heart.

Story First

Design without story is decoration. I focus on visual storytelling—where every line, pose, and shadow contributes to the narrative.

Emotion

Whether it’s a smirk, a tear, or pure chaos, I aim to capture honest, unfiltered emotion that connects with the viewer instantly.

Playfulness

I take my craft seriously—but not myself. There's always a bit of fun, wit, or surprise hidden in my work if you look close enough.

Why Collaborate With Me

You’re not just hiring an artist—you’re partnering with someone who deeply cares about character. I pour love into each design, refine until it feels right, and communicate every step of the way. Whether you’re building a comic, a game, or a story-driven brand, I’ll help your characters feel as alive to others as they do in your head.

I’m easy to talk to, allergic to fluff, and passionate about turning weird ideas into impactful visuals. Let’s make something memorable.

A Few More Words About Me

Outside the studio, I’m probably sketching in cafés, collecting old graphic novels, or imagining absurd backstories for strangers at the bus stop. I find beauty in flawed heroes, awkward pauses, and the tiny moments that make a big difference. Art isn’t just what I do—it’s how I see the world.

Bring Your Characters to Life.

Whether it’s for games, branding, or storytelling—let’s create something unforgettable. I specialize in expressive, stylized characters that connect with audiences and make brands stand out.
